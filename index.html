You wake to the sound of a scream, only to find yourself in cold and damp room deep underground. Up above you see a grate with moon light peaking through. You pick yourself up, dusting off your cloak. In the circular room there is only a lone pedestal. It sat in the center of the room, barely visible in the slivers of moonlight.

You approach the pedestal and see a crimson key. With each step you feel drawn to the beautiful key, but when the scream sounds again you regain your senses. Suddenly the key looks darker with a strange aura, a faint purple glow you hadn't notice before. Despite the ominous feeling, you grab the key, and a voice now calls to your from above.

"Do not be afriad, living one, for I am the moon. The world is in terrible danger, the hope of your kind has been taken. You have been summond here to find her before it is to late. Great dangers lie ahead. I can only see where my light touches, so I am afrid my guidence is limited. Follow your insticts, for the fate of hope is up to you. The key will allow you to enter any of the passages you encounter along your way."

The moon went quiet, offering nothing more. You look to the five passages surrounding you. Each door hidden beneath a vail of shadow. You close your eyes and listen, when a scream calls once more, you decide which path to take...

There are five doors with five unique symbols.

The Cat, The Crow, The Star, The Candle, The Book. Which do you choose?...
